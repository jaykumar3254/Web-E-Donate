Frequently Asked Questions

What is food donation?
Food donation is the act of giving excess food to those in need through NGOs and charities.
How can I donate food?
You can donate by reaching out to food banks, NGOs, or using our platform to connect with organizations in need.
Who receives the donated food?
The food is distributed to homeless shelters, orphanages, and individuals in need.
Is my food donation safe?
Yes, all donations go through quality checks before distribution to ensure safety and hygiene.
