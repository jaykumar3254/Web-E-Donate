How Our Food Donation Process Works

Our platform connects individuals with excess food to NGOs and charities that distribute it to those in need. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Request Pickup

Users can submit a food donation request through our app, providing details about the quantity and type of food.

Step 2: NGO Notification

Our system alerts nearby NGOs about the donation, enabling quick response and efficient food distribution.

Step 3: Pickup & Verification

NGO representatives collect the food, ensuring it meets quality and safety standards before distribution.

Step 4: Delivering Help

Food is distributed to homeless shelters, orphanages, and families in need, ensuring no meal goes to waste.
